Michelangelo Falvetti (December 25, 1642–1693) was an Italian Baroque composer as well as a Catholic priest . Falvetti was born in Melicuccà in Calabria , Kingdom of Naples on December 25, 1642, but spent most of his life and musical career in the Kingdom of Sicily . In 1670, he became Maestro di cappella in Palermo , and in 1678 founded the 'Unione dei Musici' in that city.
