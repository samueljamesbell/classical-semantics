Nicolas Formé (Paris 26 April 1567–27 May 1638) was a French composer. At the age of 20 in 1587 Formé joined the choir of the Sainte-Chapelle , but was excluded from the fraternity for drunkenness and womanising. He was reinstated in 1592, to the Chapelle royale , and then finally, with the support of King Louis XIII , in 1626 he returned to the Sainte-Chapelle as a canon, where he worked under the protection of the King until his death.
