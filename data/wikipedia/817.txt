Philipp Friedrich Buchner (11 September 1614 in Wertheim – 23 March 1669 in Würzburg ) was a German composer. He converted to Catholicism in Poland, and travelled to Italy to absorb the new style of Monteverdi . On his return to Germany he was employed by bishop Johann Philipp von Schönborn in Mainz for his Electoral orchestra . His works available in modern edition include a Christmas cantata .  Selections from his collection Plectrum Musicum were recorded by the ensemble Parnassi Musici and the Bavarian Chamber Orchestra for CPO in 2005.
