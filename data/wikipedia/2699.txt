Joan Baptista Pla i Agustí (ca. 1720–1773) was a Spanish composer and oboist . Pla was born in Catalonia , Spain , into a Catalan family of musicians. In the years after 1751, he worked in many of the principal cities of Europe including Padua , Stuttgart , Brussels , Paris and London along with his brother, Josep Pla i Agustí (1728-1762), a chamber musician. After Josep's death, Joan Baptista went to Lisbon as an oboist and bassoonist .  He is thought to have died in Paris . The Pla brothers left hundreds of manuscripts including about 30 trio sonatas and some concertos for flute and strings. Another brother, Manuel Pla (ca. 1725-1766) was a violinist and harpsichordist at the court of Madrid .
