Erasmus Widmann (1572–1634) was a South German composer. Widmann was born at Schwäbisch Hall , and studied in Tübingen. His first musical positions were in Eisenerz (1595), Graz (1596), Schwäbisch Hall, and from 1602 Kapellmeister to Count Wolfgang von Hohenlohe-Weikersheim till the count's death in 1610.
