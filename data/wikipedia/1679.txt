Pierre Guédron (c. 1570 in Châteaudun – ca. 1620 in Paris),  was a French singer and composer known for writing Airs de cour (including Cessés mortels de soupirer ). [ citation needed ] Guédron's Est-ce Mars (1613) was especially popular and is known in versions by Sweelinck (keyboard), Scheidt (5 part strings) and Vallet (4 lutes of different sizes).
