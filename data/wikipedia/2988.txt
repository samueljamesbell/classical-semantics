Aloys Schmitt (26 August 1788 – 25 July 1866) was a German composer , pianist and music teacher . He was born in Erlenbach am Main .  In 1824 he was appointed court composer in Munich .  He received an honorary doctorate from the University of Giessen . Among his students were Ferdinand Hiller , Carl Almenräder , Carl Arnold and Carl Wolfsohn.
