Antonín Vranický , Germanized as Anton Wranitzky , and also seen as Wranizky (June 13, 1761 in Nová Říše – August 6, 1820 in Vienna ), was a famous Czech violinist and composer of the 18th century. He was the half brother of Pavel Vranický . He was a pupil of Mozart , Haydn and Albrechtsberger , as mentioned in a letter from Pavel Vranický to the music publisher André .
