Laura Valborg Aulin (9 January 1860, Gävle – 11 January 1928, Örebro ) was a Swedish pianist and composer .  She was the sister of Tor Aulin and studied music with Benjamin Godard and Niels Wilhelm Gade .  Besides many lieder and pieces for solo piano, her compositions included two string quartets (in F major and in E minor) and organ music .
