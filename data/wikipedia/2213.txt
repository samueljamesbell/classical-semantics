Marijan Lipovšek ( Ljubljana 26 January, 1910 – 25 December, 1995) was a Slovenian composer, pianist, and teacher. A native of Ljubljana , he studied music in that city before heading to Prague in 1932; among his teachers were Pavel Šivic , Josef Suk , and Alois Hába . He later taught at the Ljubljana Academy of Music . He was father of the mezzo-soprano Marjana Lipovšek .
