Joséphine Pauline Boulay (22 May 1869 – 5 August 1925) was a French organist and composer. She was born in Paris and studied music at the Paris Conservatoire with César Franck and Jules Massenet .  She was blind and became the first woman to win a first prize in organ at the Conservatoire in 1888.  She also won first prize in Faure 's competition.
