Augustin Pfleger (1635  – after 23 July 1686) was a German composer of Bohemian birth. Pfleger was born at Schlackenwerth , now Ostrov , and became a court musician at Schlackenwerth. In 1662 he moved to the court of the Duke of Mecklenburg in Güstrow and in 1665 to Gottorf as Kapellmeister at the Schleswig-Holstein court. In 1686 he was again at Schlackenwerth, where he died.
