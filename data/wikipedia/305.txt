Carlos Berlanga , born Carlos García Berlanga , (August 11, 1959 - June 5, 2002) was a Spanish musician , composer and painter . Berlanga was born in Madrid , Spain . He was an influential figure in the Spanish music scene of the 1980s, especially in his various collaborations with the singer Alaska , such as Alaska y Dinarama and Alaska y los Pegamoides . He was the son of Luis García Berlanga , a Spanish film director .
