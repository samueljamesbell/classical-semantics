He wrote around thirty string quartets , various chamber works, operas , and orchestral pieces. He studied the violin with Carlo Chiabrano in Paris and first performed on 24 December 1756, at the Concert Spirituel , playing one of his own compositions. He also performed as first violinist in the orchestra of the Prince of Conti .  He died in Berlin at the age of 65.
