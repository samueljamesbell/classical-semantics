The Director of New Theater (Lincoln's Inn Fields, London) from 1700, Corbett was appointed orchestra director of King's Theatre , The Haymarket in 1705 and became a member of the Royal Orchestra in 1709. In 1716, he was appointed Director of the King's Band . From 1715 to 1726, Corbett lived in Italy , returning to London in 1727. It has been speculated that while abroad, Corbett was possibly acting as an agent for the Crown.
