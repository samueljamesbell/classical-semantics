Porta is believed to have been born in Venice .  One of the masters of early 18th-century opera and one of the leading Venetian musicians, Porta made his way from Rome , to Vicenza , to Verona , then London where his opera Numitore was performed in 1720 by the Royal Academy of Music (1719) , and eventually back to Venice and Verona, and finally Munich , where he spent the last 18 years of his life.
