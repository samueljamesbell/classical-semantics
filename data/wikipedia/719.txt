Christian Ludwig Boxberg ( Sondershausen 24 April 1670 – Görlitz 1 December 1729) was a German composer and organist.  From 1692 to 1700 Boxberg was active as an opera composer. His operas were performed in Leipzig , Wolfenbüttel , Kassel and Ansbach . From 1702 to 1729 he was Kapellmeister at the Church of St. Peter and Paul in Görlitz. Operas: Cantatas:
