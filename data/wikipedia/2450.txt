Moller was also an organist, concert manager, pianist, harpsichordist, and violinist.  He was born in Germany and emigrated to Philadelphia in 1790 after spending almost 10 years in England and some time in New York.  In 1796, he returned to New York where he was a manager of New York City concerts as well as serving as organist for Trinity Episcopal Church [ disambiguation needed ] .  He remained there for the rest of his life.
