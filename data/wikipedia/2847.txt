Co-creator, together with Felix Mendelssohn Bartholdy , of the first example of Romantic Organ Sonata (the first one was composed in 1845); he moved in 1847 from being organist in Merseburg cathedral to become organist in Magdeburg cathedral. In his critical writings, Ritter condemned the Renaissance organ composers referred to as the " Colorists " for overindulging in ornamentation .
