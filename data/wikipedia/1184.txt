Jan Křtitel Tolar (Latin/ German : Joannes Baptista Dolar , Czech : Jan Krtitel Tolar , also Tollar or Thollary) (c. 1620, Kamnik ) – 1673, Vienna ) was a composer and contemporary of Heinrich Ignaz Franz von Biber , Johann Heinrich Schmelzer , Andreas Hofer and Pavel Josef Vejvanovský .
