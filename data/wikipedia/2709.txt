In the 1680s he began composing operas for performance in nearby Venice . He wrote a total of 85 of them as well as 13 oratorios . His operatic style is noted for its development of arias accompanied by the orchestra and it shows some influence from the contemporary French opera of Jean-Baptiste Lully .
