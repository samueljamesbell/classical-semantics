Felix Benda (25 February 1708 – 1768) was a Bohemian composer and organist. He was not a member of the Benda musical family.    Benda was born in Skalsko .   He was a student of Bohuslav Matěj Černohorský . In 1726 he became an organist at the Michaelskirche in Prague    (succeeding Šimon Brixi ). His works, not published during his lifetime,  demonstrate his profound knowledge in counterpoint.   They include a requiem mass  organ masses, two oratorios , and other church music Benda's students included Josef Mysliveček and Josef Seger . He died in Prague .
