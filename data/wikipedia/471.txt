Sadao Bekku ( 別宮貞雄 , Bekku Sadao , May 24, 1922 in Tokyo – January 12, 2012 in Mitaka, Tokyo  ) , was a Japanese classical composer . His works include five symphonies , film scores , a flute sonata , a piano concerto , choral work and art songs , and the opera , Prince Arima . His work took strong influence from jazz . His most famous works include the film score, Matango (1963). Bekku composed about 40 film scores from 1954 to 1978.
