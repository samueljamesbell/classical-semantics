Fabrizio Dentice (also Fabricio, Fabritio) ( Naples c. 1539 – Naples c. 1581) was an Italian composer and virtuoso lute and viol player.  Fabrizio was the son of Luigi Dentice (c. 1510–1566) who served the powerful Sanseverino family and had a great reputation as a singer and lutenist.
