Giuseppe Antonio Vincenzo Aldrovandini (also Gioseffo, Giuseppe Maria, Aldovrandini, Aldrovandin, Aldrovandon, Altrobrandino,  8 June 1671 â€“ 9 February 1707) was an Italian Baroque composer . Aldovrandini was born and died in Bologna .  He is credited with writing over twenty operas and oratorios , including the 1696 opera Dafni , as well as many other instrumental compositions and arias . His other operas include L'Incoronazione di Dario, La Semiramide, Cesare in Alessandria and others. Aldrovandini composed a Magnificat in C major and a Sonata in C major.
