John Addison (c. 1765 – 30 January 1844) was a British composer and double-bass player.  Addison was born, lived, and died in London .  He wrote six operettas which were very popular at the time, including, Sacred Drama , Elijah and Songs and Glees .  He also authored a book on singing instruction, Singing Practically Treated in a Series of Instructions (1836).  Addison's song, "The Woodland Maid" was included among sixteen entries in William Alexander Barrett 's fifth volume of Standard English Songs .  " Addison, John (1766?-1844) ".
