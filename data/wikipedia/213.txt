Sven Allan Amandus Arefeldt (29 December 1908 in Stockholm , 17 August 1956 in Stockholm ), was a Swedish musician, singer and composer. Arefeldt wrote and arranged music to several films during the 1940s and 1950s.  Among Arefeldts popular recordings include " I en roddbåt till Kina ", " Hjärtats röst ", " Tre små sillar " and " Jag fick en kyss till god natt ". When Walt Disney's Alice in Wonderland (1951) was dubbed into Swedish, he gave his voice to the Cheshire Cat .
