Paul Constantinescu ( Romanian pronunciation: [ˈpa.ul konstantiˈnesku] ; 30 June 1909, Ploieşti – 20 December 1963) was a Romanian composer . Two of his main influences are Romanian folk music and Byzantine chant , both of which he used in his teaching.
