Johann Georg Ebeling (8 July 1637 – 4 December 1676) was a German composer who was born in Lüneburg and died in Stettin . Ebeling is known as editor and composer of hymns by Paul Gerhardt . He published in 1667 120 songs by Gerhardt, adding new melodies to many, writing the first melody for 26 of them.  One of his tunes is " Du meine Seele singe ". Several of his cantatas are extant.
