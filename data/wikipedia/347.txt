Pietro Baldassare or Baldassari was a Baroque composer , possibly born in Rome or Brescia , Italy about 1683. Baldassari was maestro di cappella at San Filippo Neri in Brescia from 1714 until about 1768. He was also maestro di cappella at San Clemente , Brescia until 1754. He died some time after 1768.
