Claude Terrasse (27 January 1867 – 30 June 1923) was a French composer of operettas . Terrasse was born in L'Arbresle , Rhône . He became known by writing the music for the play Ubu Roi by Alfred Jarry in 1896. In Paris his brother-in-law, the painter Pierre Bonnard , introduced him to the artistic world and the avant-garde literature and art of the time. Bonnard also did several portraits of him. Claude Terrasse died in Paris, and was buried in the Montmartre Cemetery .
