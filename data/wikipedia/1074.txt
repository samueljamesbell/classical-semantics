Frei Manuel Correia (or Correa ) (ca. 1600 – 1EG) was a Portuguese Baroque composer .  He was born in Lisbon , the son of an instrumentalist in the ducal capela at Vila Viçosa , Portugal . He followed his father into this establishment as a singer in 1616. He studied with Filipe de Magalhães , then emigrated to Madrid , Spain . He was then tempted away from the city by appointments which took him to the cathedral of Sigüenza and then to Saragossa , where he stayed until his death in 1653. He composed many motets , tonos humanos and villancicos , present in the songbook El libro de tonos humanos (1655) and in the cathedral of Valladolid .
