Peter Josef von Lindpaintner (8 December 1791 – 21 August 1856) was a German composer and conductor . Born in Koblenz as the son of a tenor , he studied with Peter Winter and Joseph Graetz . From 1819 onwards he was based in Stuttgart . Some of his early operas were Singspiele , but under the influence of Carl Maria von Weber his interest shifted to romantic opera . His best known [ citation needed ] opera Der Vampyr has similarities with Weber's Der Freischütz and the later operas of Richard Wagner .
