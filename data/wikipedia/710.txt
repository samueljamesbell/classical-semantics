Jean-Baptiste Drouard de Bousset (1662 - 3 October 1725) was a French baroque composer. He was born in Asnières , of minor nobility,  and became maître de musique of the chapelle of the Louvre .  He died in Paris . His son René Drouard de Bousset was a noted organist who also published two series of cantatas on biblical subjects. Works Recordings
