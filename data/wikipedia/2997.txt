Philipp Schöndorff or Philippus Schoendorff / Philipp Schoendorpp (1558 – after 1617) was a Flemish singer, trumpeter and composer at the court of Rudolf II under kapellmeister Philippe de Monte as a contemporary of Carl Luython , Giorgi Flori and Jacob Regnart . He was born in Liége and died in Prague .
