Henri-Jacques de Croes (baptised 19 September 1705 in Antwerp , died 16 August 1786 in Brussels  ) was a composer and violinist from the Austrian Netherlands , nowadays Belgium . His work catalogue includes several concerto grossi , masses , chamber music and a comical opera . His son Henri-Joseph de Croes (1758-1842) was also a violin player and the kapellmeister of the court orchestra of Regensburg , one of the leading orchestras of eighteenth-century Europe.
